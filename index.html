About Me

I grew up surrounded by my background of Japanese and Vietnamese heritage, which naturally shaped my love for Asian-styled design. Whether it’s traditional patterns, modern minimalism, or bold typography, I’m always exploring ways to blend cultural storytelling with digital innovation. Right now, I’m a student in Global Business and Digital Arts (GBDA) at the University of Waterloo. While GBDA is a very specific program, it’s given me a broad skillset in design, business, tech, and humanities, making me adaptable in today’s digital landscape.

I work with UI/UX design, photography, and branding. I try not to just make things look good, instead making them work seamlessly. If you’re looking for a designer who understands both aesthetics and strategy, let’s connect!
